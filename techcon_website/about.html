Our History

Founded in 2005, TechCon started as a small meetup for developers, engineers, and entrepreneurs. Today, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. Over the years, TechCon has been the stage for breakthrough innovations and groundbreaking announcements.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace Jr.

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Lovelace Jr. has advanced research in ethical AI and machine learning for healthcare. Her keynote at TechCon inspired developers to build smarter, safer, and more responsible AI systems.

Elon Tusk

An entrepreneur and innovator, Elon Tusk shared insights into renewable energy, autonomous transportation, and interplanetary travel. His vision for the future continues to inspire TechCon audiences worldwide.

Grace Hopper III

Known for her contributions to computer science education, Grace Hopper III has worked tirelessly to promote programming literacy. At TechCon, she emphasized the importance of inclusivity and accessibility in technology education.
